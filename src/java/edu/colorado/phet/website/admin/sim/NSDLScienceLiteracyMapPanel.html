NSDL Science Literacy Maps

To add our simulation to the NSDL Science Literacy maps, it will require browsing through the maps and finding keys like "SMS-MAP-0048" or "SMS-BMK-0027". Please start at http://strandmaps.nsdl.org/, select the relevant categories on the left, and browse until you find a map. The URL and page of a map will look like http://strandmaps.nsdl.org/?id=SMS-MAP-1232, and you can deduce the key "SMS-MAP-1232" from the URL. Enter in all relevant map keys.

Additionally, please browse within the maps themselves by clicking on the nodes (called "benchmarks"). If a benchmark is related to the simulation, click on it and look near the top of the pop-up box for something like 12D/M1 (ID: SMS-BMK-0830). The key "SMS-BMK-0830" should be pulled from this information at the end of the description. Adding these bookmark keys will presumably put relevant PhET simulations under the "Other Resources" or "Top Picks" for that benchmark.
